The Professional Journey

With 8.5 years of experience in high-end 3D product visualization, I specialize in transforming complex engineering data into photorealistic brand imagery. My expertise lies in the hardware and consumer electronics sectors, where I have collaborated with global industry leaders including Kohler, Dell, Haier, Whirlpool, and Elica.

I bridge the gap between industrial design and marketing by converting CAD files (STP/STEP) into compelling visual assets. Whether interpreting technical drawings to ensure design accuracy or modeling custom components to enhance realism, my focus is always on delivering a premium consumer experience.

Beyond traditional rendering, I have pioneered AR-based product models successfully integrated into e-commerce giants like Amazon, Walmart, Marks & Spencer, and Wayfair. My portfolio also extends to high-level architectural visualization, creating aesthetically refined interior environments.
